## Features

* SPI Gyro MPU6000
* On-Board OSD (controllered by Betaflight, FC over SPI bus)
* MicroSD Blackbox
* Baro (BMP280)
* F4 Processor (F405)
* 5v3a SBEC
* Built-in Current Sensor
* On-Board Video Filter (only can supply 5V to VTX and Camera)
